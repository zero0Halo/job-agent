# Steve Cover Letter Voice

## Tone

- Conversational, not corporate
- Confident without overselling
- Avoid sounding "salesy"
- Sound like an experienced engineer talking to another engineer

## Structure

- Usually 3 short paragraphs
- Strong first paragraph tailored to the company/role
- Middle paragraph grounded in actual experience
- Final paragraph brief and human

## Avoid

- Buzzwords
- Generic leadership jargon
- "Passionate about"
- "Intersection of..."
- Em dashes
- Long sentences
- Empty claims

## Prefer

- Specific technical or organizational examples
- Clear statements
- Slightly informal wording
- Concrete impact over metrics spam

## Good phrases

- "What stood out to me..."
- "I like that..."
- "I've spent a lot of time..."
- "The part of the role that caught my attention..."

## Bad phrases

- "Results-driven"
- "Dynamic leader"
- "Fast-paced environment"
- "Leveraged synergies"
